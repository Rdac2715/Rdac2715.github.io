My History

Cooper Affleck was born in Seattle Washington, 2006. He grew up in North Admiral which was a part of West Seattle. His parent were wealthy because of all of the hard work and sacrifices they made over the year prior to settiling down to have twins, Cooper and Addison. Both of his parents worked with technology and led a software developemnt company that brought them a lot of money.


During his elementary years, Cooper went to Bertschi school which was located on Capitol Hill and then in middle school to Eastside Prepatory School after his family moved to Kirkland to deal with the long commutes caused by West Seattles distance from education. Cooper is currently in his Junior year of highschool at Eastside Prepatory School and plans to major in business to achive his dream of becoming a corporate lawyer.


Over the past few years, Cooper has really come into his own and has grown into his own person. He volunteers whenever possible at one of Hopelinks many food banks. He is a member and leader of the EPS Varsity Rowing Team and has won his fair share of medals and ribbons. what's more, Cooper is a writer and editor for the Eastside Prepatory Schools school newspaper; the EPS Eagle Eye.
