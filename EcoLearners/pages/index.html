About us


We are a youth-led organization dedicated to protecting ecosystems by working with students throughout the world to ecolearn and rebuild ecosystems. With our collective knowledge, we can protect the Earth for generations to come.

Our Team


Our team consists of young students wanting to make a difference in our world. Our founders are from Kirkland, Washington and are high schoolers. Look below to learn more about us and how it all came together.

Resources


Impact your community and make a local difference. Chapters are a great way to make sure YOUR ecosystems are protected. Starting a chapter is similar to starting your own organization, and you get to decide what YOU want to do. You also get volunteer hours!


Through the chapter system, you get to brainstorm and host your own events, local biodiversity restorations, working with legislators, and working with youth at schools. You also can also plan things such as newsletters and more. It's all up to you. Create your own chapter or join one, its easy! View more down below.
